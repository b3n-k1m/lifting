Inklusive



At Inklusive we are are dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of individuals with dyslexia by providing, a model for early detection through handwriting analysis.

We understand that dyslexia is a learning difference that effects millions worldwide, and early detection is crucial for providing timely support and intervention.

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and accessible solution that empowers parents and educators to identify dyslexia at an early stage.

Inklusive understands the importance an official diagnosis from a medical professional and doesn't substitute it's need. It does however provide valuable insight, comprehensive information about dyslexia and other resources that can help it's users gain deeper understanding of the learning difference.

With inclusivity at the core of our actions, we at Inklusive hold a strong belief in the fact that by recognizing the early signs of dyslexia, we can foster a supportive environment that allows individuals to thrive in their academic and personal pursuits.
